CBE CELEBRATES 13th FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY

By IPPRC Coordinator || Date Posted: May 26, 2023

The College of Business Education celebrated its 13th Founding Anniversary on May 25-26, 2023 with the theme "13th Year of CBE's Feat in Building Resilience and Achieving Excellence" conducted at University Gymnasium. Throughout the two-day event, numerous activities were carried out. On the first day, Rev. Msgr. Quezon A. Juan officiated over a Thanksgiving Mass, which was followed by the Opening Program, where Dr. Michael M. Quilang, CEO of Top Achievers Private Schools, was the featured speaker.

On the second day, the college recognized the invaluable services, outstanding achievements, and leadership of the former Deans, as well as the students' exemplary academic performance, leadership, and cultural and athletic competition. Ms. Marilou B. Duque, a CBE benefactor, Prof. Ruby C. Sansano, and Dr. Cristina R. Salvosa, former deans of the CBE, delivered their heartwarming Inspirational Messages during the Recognition Program.

Students exhibited their innovative products at the CBE Tienda, participated in a team-building exercise, and displayed their aptitude and creativity on the CBE Runway.

Graduate students host the third Management Conference at the NVSU Mini Theatre on June 3, 2023. The centerpieces of this event are the GS Graduating Student paper presentations and the Seminar-Workshop on Research Writing, for which Dr. Caroline M. Acosta serves as the lecturer.
