Bravo, CBEshies!

By CBESC - NVSU Bayombong || Date Posted: October 6, 2023

At the recently conducted college - based ENTABLADO, the CBEshies gave a standing ovation worthy talents. From performing arts up to the literary events, the College of Business Education have it all.

The event was headed by the different student organizations of the college as Dr. Dolores Galvez, Prof, Cherrie Prelle Pascual, Prof. Jerwin Memita and Dr. Claire Reginalde gave their very inspiring messages to the CBE community and to take courage in developing their talents and skills.

The program was filled with fun and breathtaking performances from the CBEshies. Non-participants were also encouraged to showcase their talents in the ENTABLADO stage. Congratulations, College of Business Education.
